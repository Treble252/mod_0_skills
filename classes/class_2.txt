Class
Salad

Attributes
saladProtein (array)
dressingType (array)
dressingOnSide (boolean)
customerComment (string)

Methods
selectProtein (Change saladProtein to a choice of chicken, steak, or tofu; default chicken)
dressingType (Change dressingType to a choice of ranch, Italian, blue cheese, or vinegar; default ranch)
dressingOnSalad (Change dressingOnSide from true to false to put dressing on the salad)
addComment (Add a special note to the kitchen from the customer in customerComment; defaults "No Comments")
